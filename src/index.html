Thank you for visiting our website at tcotnalaska.org. The Church of The Nations is located in Anchorage, Alaska. We are a non-denominational church that is Christ-centered and inspired by the Holy Spirit.

We faithfully response to those who are hungry and homeless by reaching out and providing food, clothing and haircuts after our Sunday worship services.

We have built partnerships with members of our congregation, community churches, volunteers and donors to continue the work of Jesus by helping people in need, and proclaiming the “Good News” of the Gospel of Christ Jesus.

We believe in the freedom of worship in spirit and in truth as “The Body of Christ.” Please join us for Sunday worship service at 3:00 pm.

The Church of The Nations was established in 2008 and has been under pastoral care of Senior Pastor, Reverend Daniel Song and Associate Pastor, Reverend John Kwon. Our Lord God has sent these messengers from Korea to bring forth healings and restoration of His people through the mighty Word of God along with the presence of the Spirit of God.

Ministry and Outreach

Every quarter we invites congregations from across the world to participate in our outreach program. The main focus of our outreach is to share God's love and gopsel to those less fortunate. We visit shelters and parks. We participate in prison ministry and outreach. We help out with boys and girls clubs in the area. We also partner with our local community to provide groceries and food to our community and so much more. If you would like to experience God's love in Anchorage feel free to contact us for more information.
